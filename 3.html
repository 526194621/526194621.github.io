Plot about

GTA V

Los Santos, a huge and sunny city, is full of masters of self redemption, corrupt officials and fallen celebrities. A group of envied residents of the western world are also struggling to survive. The declining economy and cheap morality make it chaotic.
In the face of the financial turmoil, three different geeks draw their own survival and success blueprint. Franklin was a street collector, looking for real opportunities. Mike is a famous bank robber and professional criminal. He hopes to live a better life. Trev is a perverted violent and drug addict. They came together by chance. For all they want, the three choose a series of bold and dangerous criminal actions.
