PUBG

The game has multiple maps for players to choose from. In each game, 100 players participate. Players parachute to all corners of the map, find weapons, vehicles and materials with bare hands, and start battles in a variety of terrains. To win, strategy is as important as marksmanship. During the game, there will be a blue "poison circle" expelling players to a smaller place to exchange fire. In each round of the game, the safe zone will be changed randomly, and the weapons and props obtained in each zone will appear randomly.
